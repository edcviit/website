Entrepreneurship Development Cell VIIT

WHAT MAKES US EDC

Entrepreneurship Development Cell was established to push students to the forefront of the outside world but also help raise their quality and worth in the industry. To give an understanding & hands-on experience of working of an organization & its various departments contributing to the organisation's overall success. To promote, sustain, practice and manifest the spirit of entrepreneurship among individuals by providing them a platform to convert their ideas into successful ventures.

OUR INITIATIVES

Vishwapreneur

Vishwapreneur

Vishwapreneur is a National Level Entrepreneurial Summit showcasing entrepreneurs, creators and innovative minds of our century who had the courage to think out of the box. Vishwapreneur is a platform for budding entrepreneurs to seek motivation & guidance through experiences of successful business tycoon.

Agnitio'22

Agnitio'22

"Agnitio", The B-Plan Competition emerged with the "Ideation Phase" resulting into many Start-Up ideas.

E-Cell Meet

E-Cell Meet

Ecell Meet-up is a networking platform for aspiring entrepreneurs and growing individuals with similar mindsets.Ecells join to learn more about the structure, operations and working. A wide spectrum of proper guidance and mentorship for different start-up ideas is provided which helps in achieving our goal of fostering entrepreneurship through seminars and panel discussions.All these scattered lights collectively give rise to the Zenith of Development in Entrepreneurship.

Corpstrata

Corpstrata

Corpstrata’ comes from Corporate Strategies. In order to introduce the entrepreneurial concepts in an easy and playful manner, Corpstrata comes into picture. From campaigning to documenting and from getting sponsors to learning how to finance that money, Corpstrata exposes the first-year students to everything.

Student Facilitation Center

Student Facilitation Center

As aptly named, Student Facilitation Centre’s first and foremost aim is to facilitate the students. The Student Facilitation Centre provides the students with all the necessary items required for their first year of engineering, in the form of a basic kit. Ever since its inception, it has been known to be highly convenient. .

ACTIVITIES AT A GLANCE

TUG OF WORDS

The debate was conducted in two rounds with two different topics in each round. The first topic was “Is Peer Pressure beneficial or harmful”. It was followed by the next round with the topic “Service based or Product based startups”.

EDC WANTS TO KNOW

We simulated a press conference atmosphere in our cell where all the people in the cell had different roles depending on the given situation.

BIDDING WARS

The activity was organized to providing the par experience of the grand IPL auction. All the participants were divided into 8 teams. The teams were given points according to which they bid on various players and formed a team of six players.

PITCH DESK

Members were divided into teams and a common problem statement was given to teams. The team members had to find out an innovative solution to the problem given.The participants were judged based on their creative ideas to provide a real time solution to the given problem statement.

MARKET MANIA

This activity which took us through the very important phase of any startup which is marketing strategies. Marketing Strategies involves various kinds of techniques, but it is totally up to the team members as to what techniques they rely on to make their strategy unique and effective.

₹100 ENTREPRENEUR

In this activity, we indulge our members to make utmost proﬁts out of the least possible investments.
