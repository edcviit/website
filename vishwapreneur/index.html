FAQ's

What is Vishwapreneur?
Vishwapreneur is a National Level Entrepreneurial Summit showcasing entrepreneurs, creators and innovative minds of our century who had the courage to think out of the box. Vishwapreneur is a platform for budding entrepreneurs to seek motivation & guidance through the experiences of successful business tycoons.
How many events will be there in Vishwapreneur?
Two
Is there any registration fee for the events?
Yes! “Good things in life don't come for free “ there will be a bare minimum fee of ₹200
How to register for the event?
You can register through our website www.vishwapreneur.in
Who can participate in the event?
Anyone and everyone interested in entrepreneurship and wants to do something different from the rest of the people should participate in the event.
In what mode will the events be held?
The event will be held in online mode.
What is the duration of the events?
The event will be held for 2 days.
Can we participate in more than one event?
Yes!
