Corpstrata


Corpstrata as the name suggests 'Coporate-Strategy' is event of fun and learning where student participate in activities to inculcate entrepreneurial skills in them and get in touch with corporate world.

Acridd Hunt

Crack the Clues or you lose

It is the mixture of acting and riddles, basically it's a treasure hunt. In which you'll get to know about many companies through their tagline.

Eliminator-X

Play in your world, get eliminated in ours.

This is a physical activity with some twisted quiz which will give you a short experience of Roadies.

CORP-G

Winner Winner Corporate Leader

This is a real-life PUBG with a touch a Corporate World and gives a glimpse of the part to become a Corporate Leader.

Wall Street Survivor

Be the Wolf of Wall Street

This activity gives you a short experience of Share Market and how to invest with handling roumours.

Quiddle

Outwit. Outplay. Outsmart.

It is a combination of Quiz and Riddle where you'll learn some Entrepreneural skills.

Labyrinth of Unknown

The only way out is Knowledge

It a physical activity similar to treasure hunt, where you'll have to search for a products through hidden clues.

Bid Buy Bid

Trade, Bid & Enjoy

It is a corporate Auction where you have to bid for Companies & CEO's in given fixed amount.

Blind Maze

The only way lies within

It is a combination of Maze game and Blind fold, where you'll have to search for Companies through maze while being blind folded.

Hunt-repreneur

Think Smart, every move counts.

This is a board game similar to Scotland-yard game where you'll have to Hunt for Entrepreneurs through game.

Hurdlon

Across a dozen dimensions.

It is fun based activity where you'll get hand to hand experience of overall journey of startup.
